How to Build a Simple Garden Bed

Building a garden bed is easier than you think! Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you grow your own veggies, herbs, or flowers right at home.

What You’ll Need:

  • Wood boards (cedar or fir are great options)
  • Screws or corner brackets
  • A drill or screwdriver
  • Weed barrier fabric (optional)
  • Good quality soil or raised bed mix
  • Plants or seeds of your choice

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Pick a Sunny Spot
  2. Decide Your Size
  3. Tools You’ll Need:
    • Wood boards
    • Screws
    • Drill
  4. Build the Frame

Extra Tips:

  • Add mulch to help retain moisture.
  • Install a drip hose or soaker hose for easy watering.
  • Rotate crops each season to keep your soil healthy.
